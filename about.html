About

The Academic Conference is an international gathering of researchers, professionals, and experts in the field of AI and Software Engineering. The conference aims to foster collaboration, exchange knowledge, and promote advancements in reliable and secure AI for software engineering.

Attendees will have the opportunity to participate in keynote speeches, panel discussions, paper presentations, and workshops led by renowned speakers and researchers in the industry. The conference will cover various topics such as AI applications, ethical considerations, code analysis, and more.

Join us at the Academic Conference to explore the latest developments in AI for software engineering and network with peers and experts in the field.
